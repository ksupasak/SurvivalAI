✅  Short version: SurvivalAI is built around a simple principle — your survival data belongs to you. Everything you enter (medical profile, settings, chat history) is stored only on your device. We have no server, no database, and no knowledge of who you are.

01 — Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how SurvivalAI ("we", "our", "us") handles information when you use the SurvivalAI mobile application ("App") and this website ("Site"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and have designed SurvivalAI from the ground up to minimize data exposure.

This policy complies with applicable data protection regulations including the Personal Data Protection Act B.E. 2562 (PDPA) of Thailand and other applicable privacy laws.

02 — Data We Do NOT Collect

To be explicit about our privacy-first approach, here is what we never collect:

  • Your name, email address, phone number, or any identity information
  • Your location or GPS coordinates (these remain on-device only)
  • Your personal emergency profile (medical conditions, blood type, allergies, etc.)
  • Your chat history with the AI assistant
  • Camera or microphone recordings
  • Usage analytics, crash reports, or behavioral data
  • Device identifiers (IMEI, advertising ID, etc.)
  • BLE beacon broadcasts or the data contained within them
  • Cookies or tracking pixels (on the App; see website section below)

03 — Data Stored on Your Device

The following information is stored locally on your device only and is never transmitted to our servers:

Data Type Purpose Storage
Emergency Profile Name, blood type, medical conditions, allergies, medications, emergency contacts — for BLE beacon and Morse transmission when you initiate it Local Only
App Settings Theme, AI mode, voice preferences, sensitivity settings Local Only
Downloaded Manuals Offline government survival documents you choose to download Local Only
Downloaded AI Model Optional quantized LLM file (~2–4 GB) for offline chat Local Only
Chat History Conversation history with the AI assistant (if enabled) Local Only

You can delete all locally stored data at any time by clearing the App's data in your device settings or by uninstalling the App.

04 — Third-Party Services

SurvivalAI is primarily offline. However, certain optional features may interact with third-party services:

Service When Used Data Sent
OpenAI API (ChatGPT) Only when you select "Online AI" mode and have network access Your chat message text. Subject to OpenAI's Privacy Policy
Google Fonts When loading the promotional website only (not the App) Your IP address (standard web font loading)
App Store / Google Play When downloading the App Subject to Apple's / Google's respective privacy policies

The online AI feature is opt-in and clearly labeled in the App. When you use the offline knowledge base or on-device LLM mode, no data leaves your device. We recommend offline mode in sensitive situations.

05 — BLE Beacon (Seeking Mode)

When you activate Seeking Mode, the App broadcasts a Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE) beacon signal. This is a one-way transmission — the App does not receive or store data from devices that detect the beacon.

The beacon may include:

  • A unique service identifier for SurvivalAI rescue detection
  • Selected fields from your Emergency Profile (only fields you have filled in)
  • Your GPS coordinates (if location permission is granted)

This information is transmitted wirelessly to nearby devices with compatible rescue scanning software. You control when this beacon is active — it only broadcasts when Seeking Mode is explicitly turned on. We have no access to these transmissions.

06 — Camera and Microphone

Camera: Used exclusively for real-time light detection in Morse code Read mode. The camera feed is processed in real time on your device. No images or video are captured, stored, or transmitted by SurvivalAI.

Microphone: Used for voice-to-text input in the AI chat. Audio is converted to text on-device (using your device's built-in speech recognition) or through your chosen AI provider's API if you are using the online AI mode. SurvivalAI does not record, store, or transmit audio.

07 — This Website

The SurvivalAI promotional website (this site) is a static HTML page hosted without analytics or tracking scripts. We do not use:

  • Google Analytics or any analytics platform
  • Advertising pixels or retargeting scripts
  • Cookies beyond what browsers set automatically
  • Session tracking or fingerprinting

The only third-party request made by this website is loading fonts from Google Fonts, which transmits your IP address to Google as part of the font delivery. You can block this in your browser's developer tools if desired.

08 — PDPA Compliance (Thailand)

SurvivalAI is designed to be compliant with the Personal Data Protection Act B.E. 2562 (PDPA) of Thailand. Because we do not collect, process, or store personal data on any server, the data controller obligations under PDPA are significantly limited.

Key points:

  • No consent is required for data we never collect
  • Data stored on your device is under your sole control
  • You may exercise your PDPA rights (access, erasure, portability) by clearing App data on your device
  • For online AI features, the relevant data controller is the AI provider (e.g., OpenAI); their privacy policy governs those interactions

For more information about PDPA, visit the official PDPA website.

09 — Children's Privacy

SurvivalAI does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13 years of age. The App is designed for use by adults in emergency preparedness scenarios. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided personal information through the App's online features, please contact us so we can address it.

10 — Data Security

Because data is stored locally on your device, the security of your information depends on your device's security (PIN, biometrics, encryption). We recommend:

  • Using a strong PIN or biometric lock on your device
  • Enabling device encryption (standard on modern iOS and Android)
  • Being mindful of what you enter in your Emergency Profile, as it may be transmitted via BLE when Seeking Mode is active

11 — Your Rights

You have full control over your data:

  • Access: All your data is visible within the App itself
  • Correction: Edit any profile field at any time within the App
  • Deletion: Clear all data by uninstalling the App or clearing its storage in device settings
  • Portability: Your profile data is stored in standard device storage and can be accessed via device backup tools
  • Withdraw consent: Revoke camera, microphone, location, or Bluetooth permissions at any time through your device's system settings

12 — Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically. For significant changes, we will include a notice in the App on next launch.

13 — Contact

If you have questions, concerns, or requests related to your privacy, please open an issue on the project's public repository or reach out through the official SurvivalAI website. We take privacy concerns seriously and will respond promptly.
