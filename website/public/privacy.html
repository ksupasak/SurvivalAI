Section 01

Who We Are

This Privacy Policy describes how the SurvivalAI development team ("we", "us", "our") handles information in connection with the SurvivalAI mobile application (com.survivalai.app, v1.0.0) on iOS and Android, and the associated promotional website.

This policy is written in compliance with the Personal Data Protection Act B.E. 2562 (PDPA) of Thailand and reflects applicable international privacy standards. Because we collect no personal data on any server, our obligations as a data controller are minimal — but we document our practices fully for your transparency.

Section 02

Data We Never Collect

The following information is never collected, transmitted, or stored by SurvivalAI on any server or third-party system under our control:

  • Your name, email address, phone number, or any identity credentials
  • Your Emergency Profile content (medical conditions, blood type, allergies, medications, emergency contacts)
  • Your GPS or location coordinates
  • Camera footage, images, or video from your device
  • Audio recordings or microphone data
  • AI chat history or conversation content
  • BLE beacon transmissions or their content
  • App usage patterns, session duration, or feature usage analytics
  • Crash reports or diagnostic telemetry
  • Device identifiers (IMEI, advertising ID, IDFA, Android ID)
  • IP address or network information (App)

We have deliberately built no server infrastructure that could receive this data.

Section 05

BLE Beacon — Seeking Mode

When you activate Seeking Mode, the App broadcasts a Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE) advertisement packet. This is important to understand:

  • One-way broadcast only — the App does not receive data from, or connect to, any other device
  • You control the content — only fields you have filled in your Emergency Profile are included; empty fields are excluded
  • You control the timing — the beacon is only active when you explicitly turn on Seeking Mode; it stops the moment you turn it off or close the App
  • Nearby devices may detect it — any BLE scanner within range (~10–50 m) can detect the advertisement. This is intentional — it is the rescue-detection mechanism
  • We cannot see it — we have no infrastructure that receives or logs BLE broadcasts

Because BLE broadcasts are wireless and inherently detectable by nearby devices, activate Seeking Mode only when you intend to signal for rescue.

Section 07

This Website

The SurvivalAI promotional website is a fully static HTML site served via Cloudflare Pages. It contains no:

  • Analytics scripts (Google Analytics, Mixpanel, Plausible, etc.)
  • Advertising pixels or retargeting code
  • Session tracking or fingerprinting
  • Contact forms that submit personal data
  • Cookies set by us

The only third-party request is loading fonts from Google Fonts (fonts.googleapis.com), which sends your IP address to Google as part of the HTTP font-delivery request. If you wish to avoid this, you may block fonts.googleapis.com in your browser or network-level DNS filter.

Cloudflare Pages may log access requests (IP, URL, timestamp) as part of their standard CDN infrastructure. This is governed by Cloudflare's Privacy Policy.

Section 08

PDPA Compliance (Thailand)

SurvivalAI is designed to be compliant with the Personal Data Protection Act B.E. 2562 (PDPA) of Thailand. Because we collect no personal data on any server, the majority of PDPA controller obligations do not apply to our operations.

  • No consent required from us — we collect nothing, so no lawful basis is needed for collection
  • You are the sole controller of data stored on your device
  • Access & erasure — exercisable by you directly within the App or via device settings
  • Third-party AI providers — when online AI is used, those providers are independent data controllers governed by their own PDPA/GDPR compliance posture

For further information about Thailand's PDPA, visit the Personal Data Protection Committee (PDPC) official website.

Section 09

Children's Privacy

SurvivalAI is intended for users aged 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. Because we collect no data at all through server infrastructure, there is no mechanism by which a child could submit personal data to us through the App itself.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has used the optional online AI feature and sent data to a third-party AI provider, please review that provider's policies and parental controls directly.

Section 10

Data Security

Since all your data lives on your device, its security is tied to your device's security posture. We recommend:

  • Enable a strong PIN, password, or biometric lock on your device
  • Enable full-device encryption (enabled by default on modern iOS and Android)
  • Be aware that your Emergency Profile content is transmitted wirelessly via BLE when Seeking Mode is active — activate it only when you intend to signal for help
  • If you use online AI mode, treat your AI provider API key like a password — store it nowhere else and revoke it if your device is lost

We have no server-side data to secure or breach. If your device is lost or stolen, your data exposure is limited to what is on that device.
